A plain-English inbox for support tickets.

When something breaks — a mapping fails, an integration drops messages, a customer escalates — it lands here as a ticket. Each row below is a short note about what is wrong, who reported it, and how urgent it is.

Open a ticket to read the full context. From there you can run AI actions to classify it, find similar past issues, draft a reply, or build a formal change request. The AI proposes; a human reviews before anything goes out.
